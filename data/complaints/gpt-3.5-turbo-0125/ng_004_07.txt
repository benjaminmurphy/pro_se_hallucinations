COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Jessica Morales ("Plaintiff"), by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant Tracy Hargrove ("Defendant"), alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Jessica Morales, is an individual residing at [Your Address], [City], Massachusetts.

2. Defendant, Tracy Hargrove, is the owner of 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA, where the incident giving rise to this action occurred.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

3. On or about February 15, 2023, at 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA, Plaintiff was delivering groceries to a tenant of the property, when she slipped and fell on the icy sidewalk maintained by Defendant.

4. Despite recent snowfall, Defendant negligently failed to adequately clear the sidewalk of snow and ice, creating a hazardous condition.

5. Plaintiffs' fall on the ice caused her to suffer a lower back injury that required medical treatment, resulting in approximately $1,500 in medical expenses and $1,000 in lost wages due to missed work.

6. Prior to the incident, tenants at the property had informed Defendant about the dangerous icy conditions, providing notice and warning of the hazardous situation resulting from Defendant's negligence.

7. Despite receiving notice from tenants, Defendant callously disregarded the warnings and failed to take appropriate action to remedy the hazardous condition.

CAUSE OF ACTION

8. The Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain the premises in a reasonably safe condition, including clearing snow and ice from the sidewalk, to prevent harm to individuals lawfully on the property.

9. Defendant breached this duty by negligently failing to properly maintain the sidewalk, resulting in the hazardous condition that caused Plaintiffs' injuries.

10. Defendant's negligence directly and proximately caused Plaintiffs' injuries and resulting damages.

11. As a direct and proximate result of Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff suffered physical injuries, pain and suffering, medical expenses, and lost wages.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court enter judgment in her favor and against Defendant, Tracy Hargrove, and award:

1. Compensatory damages in the amount of $2,500 for medical expenses and lost wages;
2. Prejudgment and post-judgment interest as allowed by law;
3. Costs and attorney fees incurred in prosecuting this action; and
4. Such further relief as the Court deems just and proper.

JURY DEMAND

Plaintiff requests a trial by jury on all triable issues.
