**COMPLAINT**

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jake Williams, by and through undersigned counsel, and for his Complaint against the Defendant, Mark Johnson, hereby alleges as follows:

**I. PARTIES**

1. The Plaintiff, Jake Williams, is an individual residing at [Plaintiff's Address], Boston, MA.
 
2. The Defendant, Mark Johnson, is an individual upon whom process can be served, residing at [Defendant's Address], Boston, MA.

**II. JURISDICTION AND VENUE**

3. This Honorable Court has jurisdiction over this matter as the incident giving rise to this action occurred in Suffolk County, Massachusetts.

4. Venue is proper in Suffolk County Superior Court as the alleged actions and omissions leading to the cause of action occurred within its jurisdiction.

**III. FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS**

5. On September 10, 2023, around [Time of Incident], the Plaintiff was lawfully operating his bicycle in a marked bike lane located at 500 Commonwealth Ave, Boston, MA.

6. The Defendant, Mark Johnson, while parked in a loading zone adjacent to the bike lane, negligently and recklessly opened the door of his vehicle without checking for oncoming traffic, causing the Plaintiff to collide with the door and crash onto the pavement.

7. The Plaintiff sustained injuries to his arms and legs resulting in road rash, a chipped front tooth, and damage to his bicycle frame as a direct result of the Defendant's careless and negligent actions.

8. Despite admitting to distracted driving and acknowledging his failure to see the Plaintiff due to checking his phone, the Defendant later provided false and defamatory information to his insurance company, contradicting the initial admission.

9. The Plaintiff incurred approximately $1,200 in medical expenses for necessary dental work and suffered a financial loss of approximately $600 in lost wages due to the injuries sustained in the incident.

**IV. CAUSES OF ACTION**

10. The Defendant's actions constitute negligence under the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. The Defendant breached their duty of care owed to the Plaintiff by failing to exercise reasonable care and caution when opening the vehicle door, thereby causing the Plaintiff's injuries.

**V. PRAYER FOR RELIEF**

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court:

11. Awards compensatory damages in the amount of $1,800 for medical expenses and lost wages incurred by the Plaintiff as a direct result of the Defendant's negligence.

12. Awards any further relief as the Court deems just and proper.

**VI. JURY DEMAND**

13. The Plaintiff demands a trial by jury on all issues so triable as a matter of right.
