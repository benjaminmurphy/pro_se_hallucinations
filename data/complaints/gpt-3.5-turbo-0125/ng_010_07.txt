FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On August 15, 2023, at around [time], Plaintiff was walking on the sidewalk by 123 Maple Street, Pittsfield, MA 01201.

6. Defendant, as the owner of the property at 123 Maple Street, was negligent in maintaining a large maple tree on the property.

7. Prior to the incident, a significant branch of the tree was visibly cracked and drooping over the sidewalk, posing a danger to pedestrians.

8. Despite being informed by neighbors that the cracked branch needed attention and inspection by an arborist, Defendant negligently failed to take any action to address the hazardous condition.

9. During a normal summer thunderstorm on August 15, 2023, the cracked branch finally snapped and fell on Plaintiff, causing her to sustain a shoulder strain and multiple bruises.

10. As a direct and proximate result of Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff incurred approximately $1,200 in medical expenses and lost approximately $500 in wages due to missed work.

CAUSES OF ACTION

11. Defendant breached the duty of care owed to Plaintiff by failing to inspect, maintain, and address the hazardous condition posed by the cracked tree branch, which resulted in injuries to Plaintiff.

12. Defendant's negligence in failing to address the dangerous condition of the tree branch directly caused the injuries and damages suffered by Plaintiff.
