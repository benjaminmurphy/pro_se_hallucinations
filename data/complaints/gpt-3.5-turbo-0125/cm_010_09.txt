COMPLAINT FOR MODIFICATION OF CUSTODY

COMES NOW, the Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson, by and through the undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint for Modification of Custody against the Defendant, Michael Johnson, states as follows:

PARTIES AND JURISDICTION

1. The Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson, is a resident of Middlesex County, Massachusetts.
2. The Defendant, Michael Johnson, is also a resident of Middlesex County, Massachusetts.
3. The child subject to this action is Sophie Johnson, who is currently ten years old.
4. The original custody order was entered on May 15, 2016, in the Middlesex County Probate and Family Court.
5. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 209, Section 37.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

6. The original custody order granted primary residence to the Defendant, Michael Johnson, with limited weekend visits and one weeknight dinner to the Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson.
7. The basis for the original custody arrangement was Plaintiff's then enrollment in grad school, limited living space, and restrictive schedule.
8. Since the entry of the original custody order, significant changes have occurred. Plaintiff has completed her education, secured stable employment with regular hours, and now resides in a two-bedroom apartment where Sophie has her own room.
9. Sophie Johnson has expressed a desire for more time with Plaintiff. In a letter to the Court and through discussions with her therapist, Sophie has indicated feeling guilty about short visits and has requested increased time spent with her mother.
10. Despite Sophie's expressed wishes, Defendant, Michael Johnson, has been uncooperative and refuses to consider modifying the custody schedule.
11. It is in the best interest of Sophie Johnson to have an updated custody arrangement that allows for more meaningful time with her mother.

REQUESTED MODIFICATION

12. Plaintiff respectfully requests this Honorable Court to modify the existing custody order to grant increased parenting time to the Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson. Specifically, Plaintiff seeks an updated arrangement that allows for additional overnights and more quality time with Sophie Johnson.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Thompson respectfully requests this Honorable Court grant the following relief:

1. Modify the existing custody order to allow for increased parenting time for the Plaintiff.
2. Such other and further relief as the Court deems just and equitable.
