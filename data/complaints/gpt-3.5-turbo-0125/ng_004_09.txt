COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Jacob Adams, by and through undersigned counsel, and for his Complaint against Defendant Linda Greene, respectfully states as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Jacob Adams is an individual residing at [Your Address], [City], MA.

2. Defendant Linda Greene is the owner of the property located at 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises under the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 218, Section 21.

4. Venue is proper in Worcester District Court as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On February 15, 2023, at 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA, Plaintiff Jacob Adams was delivering groceries to a tenant of the property.

6. Defendant Linda Greene, as the owner and responsible party for the maintenance of the property, negligently failed to properly clear the sidewalk of snow and ice following a snowstorm.

7. Despite being informed by the tenants of the hazardous ice conditions on the sidewalk, Defendant Greene failed to take appropriate action to remedy the dangerous situation.

8. As a result of Defendant Greene's negligence, Plaintiff Adams slipped and fell on the icy sidewalk, injuring his lower back and suffering damages.

CAUSES OF ACTION

9. Plaintiff incorporates paragraphs 1 through 8 as if fully set forth herein.

10. Defendant Greene owed a duty of care to maintain the sidewalk in a safe condition for pedestrians under Massachusetts negligence law.

11. Defendant Greene breached this duty by negligently clearing the snow but failing to address the resulting dangerous ice conditions promptly.

12. The breach of Defendant Greene's duty of care directly caused Plaintiff Adams' injuries and resulting damages.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jacob Adams respectfully requests that this Court enter judgment in his favor and against Defendant Linda Greene, and grant the following relief:

A. Compensatory damages in the amount of $3,300, which includes medical expenses of approximately $2,500 and lost wages of approximately $800;
B. Prejudgment interest as allowed by law;
C. Costs and attorneys' fees incurred in this action;
D. Any further relief that this Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff hereby demands a trial by jury on all issues so triable.
