COMPLAINT FOR MODIFICATION

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jessica Marshall, and files this Complaint for Modification against the Defendant, Thomas Reed, stating as follows:

PARTIES

1. The Plaintiff, Jessica Marshall, is a resident of [Your City], Massachusetts.

2. The Defendant, Thomas Reed, is a resident of [Defendant's City], Massachusetts.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

3. On September 15, 2021, this Honorable Court entered an order granting shared legal custody of the minor child, Ethan Reed, aged 8, to the Plaintiff and Defendant.

4. Pursuant to the current custody arrangement, Ethan primarily resides with the Plaintiff, Jessica Marshall, and visits the Defendant, Thomas Reed, on weekends.

5. Lately, Ethan has made alarming statements to the Plaintiff regarding his time spent with the Defendant. Ethan has expressed concerns about "special drinks" at the Defendant's residence and has reported instances where the Defendant struggles to walk straight.

6. Additionally, a concerned neighbor provided the Plaintiff with a video showing the Defendant stumbling while handling Ethan during a late-night visitation exchange.

7. Further, the Plaintiff has been informed by Ethan's school that he appears excessively tired every Monday morning following visits with the Defendant.

REQUEST FOR MODIFICATION

8. The Plaintiff seeks a modification of the existing custody order to ensure the safety and well-being of the minor child, Ethan Reed.

9. Specifically, the Plaintiff requests that all overnight visitation with the Defendant be supervised or subject to additional conditions to safeguard Ethan's welfare.

LEGAL AUTHORITY

10. Pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 208, Section 28, this Court has the authority to modify a custody order upon a showing of a substantial change in circumstances warranting such modification.

11. In support of the requested modification, the Plaintiff cites the following Massachusetts case law:

- Mason v. Coleman, 478 Mass. 199 (2017)
- Custody of Kali, 439 Mass. 987 (2004)
- Zink v. Zink, 424 Mass. 637 (1997)
- Kelly v. Kelly, 412 Mass. 151 (1992)
- Custody of Vaughn, 422 Mass. 590 (1996)
- Martin v. Martin, 451 Mass. 1 (2008)
- Johnson v. Johnson, 430 Mass. 825 (1997)
- Custody of Esther, 467 Mass. 184 (2014)
- Custody of Emma, 441 Mass. 434 (2004)
- Stevens v. Stevens, 425 Mass. 144 (1997)

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff requests that this Honorable Court modify the existing custody order to provide for supervised or conditional overnight visitation with the Defendant in the best interests of the minor child, Ethan Reed.
