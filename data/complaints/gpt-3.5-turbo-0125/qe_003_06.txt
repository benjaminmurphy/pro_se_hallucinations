COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Cameron Lee, and for his Complaint against the Defendant, Jordan McCarthy, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. The Plaintiff, Cameron Lee, is an individual residing at 123 Green Street, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130.

2. The Defendant, Jordan McCarthy, is the landlord of the aforementioned residential premises.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises under the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, including but not limited to the Massachusetts General Laws governing landlord-tenant relationships.

4. This Court has jurisdiction over the subject matter of this action as the amount in controversy exceeds the minimum threshold required for Housing Court jurisdiction.

5. Venue is proper in Boston Housing Court as the leased premises are located at 123 Green Street, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

6. On January 15, 2022, Plaintiff entered into a lease agreement with Defendant to rent a studio apartment located at 123 Green Street, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130, for a monthly rent of $1500.

7. During the showing of the apartment prior to entering into the lease agreement, Plaintiff noticed water stains on the ceiling in the apartment. Defendant assured Plaintiff that the water damage had been completely repaired.

8. Despite the assurances made by Defendant, water started dripping through the same spot on the ceiling during the first significant rainstorm after Plaintiff moved in.

9. Plaintiff has repeatedly notified Defendant of the ongoing water leakage issue, but Defendant has failed to adequately address the problem, dismissing it as a "minor cosmetic issue."

10. The water leakage issue has worsened over time, causing damage to Plaintiff's personal property including a couch and a laptop, resulting in $900 in damages to Plaintiff.

11. The water leakage has led to a musty smell and potential mold growth in the apartment, impacting Plaintiff's health and well-being.

CAUSE OF ACTION

12. Plaintiff asserts a cause of action for breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment under Massachusetts law. The covenant of quiet enjoyment guarantees tenants the right to live in a rented premises free from interference and disturbances by the landlord.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court:

a. Award damages to Plaintiff in the amount of $900 for the property damage suffered;
b. Order Defendant to immediately repair the water leakage issue in the leased premises;
c. Award any further relief that the Court deems just and proper.
