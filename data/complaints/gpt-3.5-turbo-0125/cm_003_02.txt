Complaint for Modification - Custody Order

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jessica Martinez, and for her Complaint against Defendant, Michael Thompson, states as follows:

1. The Parties
1.1 Plaintiff Jessica Martinez is a resident of [Your Address], and the mother of Ethan Thompson, age 8.
1.2 Defendant Michael Thompson is a resident of [Defendant's Address].

2. Background and Jurisdiction
2.1 On September 15, 2021, this Honorable Court entered an Order granting the parties shared legal custody of Ethan Thompson.
2.2 This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 208, Section 28, which permits modification of custody orders when there has been a material change in circumstances.

3. Factual Allegations
3.1 Since the entry of the custody order, Plaintiff has observed concerning behavior and conditions during Ethan's visits with Defendant.
3.2 Ethan has informed Plaintiff of the presence of "special drinks" at Defendant's residence and occasions when Defendant "can't walk straight."
3.3 A neighbor has provided Plaintiff with a video showing Defendant stumbling while getting Ethan out of the car late at night.
3.4 Additionally, the school has reported that Ethan appears exhausted every Monday morning following his visits to Defendant's residence.

4. Requested Modification
4.1 Plaintiff requests that this Court modify the existing custody order to ensure Ethan's safety and well-being during visits with Defendant.
4.2 Specifically, Plaintiff seeks supervised overnights or any other conditions deemed necessary by the Court for the protection and best interests of Ethan.

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Martinez respectfully requests this Honorable Court to:
a) Modify the existing custody order to ensure Ethan's safety during visits with Defendant; and
b) Grant such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.

Respectfully submitted,
