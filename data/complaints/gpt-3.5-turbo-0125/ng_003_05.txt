COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff David Thompson, by and through undersigned counsel, and for his Complaint against HomeGoods Discount Store (hereinafter “Defendant”), alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff David Thompson is an individual residing at [Your Address], City of [Your City], State of Massachusetts.

2. Defendant HomeGoods Discount Store is a business entity operating in Massachusetts with a place of business located at 123 Market Street, Lowell, MA.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises under the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

4. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter based on the diversity of citizenship of the parties and the amount in controversy exceeding the statutory minimum.

5. Venue is proper in the Middlesex Superior Court as the events giving rise to the action occurred at 123 Market Street, Lowell, MA.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

6. On or about August 15, 2023, at HomeGoods Discount Store located at 123 Market Street, Lowell, MA, Plaintiff was browsing the kitchen appliance aisle.

7. Defendant had negligently stacked heavy boxes of appliances without proper securing measures, with approximately five to six boxes stacked high on shelves.

8. Another customer, while pushing a cart down the aisle, inadvertently bumped into a shelf, causing a box containing a toaster-oven to fall from the top shelf, striking Plaintiff on the shoulder and side of the head.

9. The impact from the falling box caused Plaintiff to suffer a mild concussion and a shoulder strain, leading to subsequent medical treatment and financial losses.

CAUSES OF ACTION

10. Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain a safe shopping environment for its customers under Massachusetts negligence law.

11. Defendant breached its duty by negligently stacking heavy boxes without proper securing measures, resulting in the box falling on and injuring Plaintiff.

12. As a direct and proximate result of Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff suffered bodily injuries, medical expenses, and economic losses.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff David Thompson respectfully requests that this Court grant the following relief:

a. Compensatory damages in an amount to be determined at trial but estimated at $1,200 for medical expenses and $600 for lost wages,
  
b. Prejudgment and post-judgment interest,
  
c. Costs and attorneys' fees, and
  
d. Any further relief as the Court deems just and proper.
