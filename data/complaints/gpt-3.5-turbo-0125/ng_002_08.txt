COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jessica Turner, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant Mark Sullivan, alleges and states as follows:

1. Parties
1.1. Plaintiff, Jessica Turner, is an individual residing at [Plaintiff’s Address], Newton, MA.
1.2. Defendant, Mark Sullivan, is an individual residing at [Defendant’s Address], Newton, MA.

2. Jurisdiction and Venue
2.1. This Court has jurisdiction over the subject matter of this action pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 218, § 185.
2.2. Venue is proper in this Court as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at Parks and Recreation Trails, 45 Park Ave, Newton, MA, which is within the jurisdiction of this Court.

3. Factual Allegations
3.1. On September 15, 2023, at Parks and Recreation Trails in Newton, MA, Plaintiff was jogging on the park path.
3.2. Defendant's large dog, off-leash despite park regulations requiring all dogs to be leashed, charged at Plaintiff, barking loudly.
3.3. Despite being approximately thirty feet away, Defendant failed to control his dog by not having it on a leash, causing Plaintiff to fear for her safety.
3.4. In an attempt to avoid the charging dog, Plaintiff swerved off the path and tripped over a tree root, falling down a small embankment and fracturing her wrist.

4. Causes of Action
4.1. Negligence - Defendant owed a duty of care to keep his dog on a leash in a public park. Defendant breached this duty by allowing his dog to be off-leash, causing Plaintiff's injuries. This breach of duty was the proximate cause of Plaintiff's injuries and damages.

5. Prayer for Relief
WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Turner respectfully requests that this Honorable Court grant the following relief:
5.1. Award compensatory damages in the amount of $4,700, representing approximately $3,200 for medical expenses and $1,500 for lost wages.
5.2. Award any further relief as the Court deems just and proper.
5.3. Grant such other and further relief as this Court deems just and proper.

6. Jury Trial Demand
Plaintiff demands trial by jury on all issues so triable.
