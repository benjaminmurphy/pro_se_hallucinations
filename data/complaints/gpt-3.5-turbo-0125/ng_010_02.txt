COMPLAINT

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Jessica Carter, is a resident of Pittsfield, Massachusetts.

2. Defendant, Thomas Reynolds, is the owner of the property located at 123 Maple Street, Pittsfield, MA 01201.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 218, Section 3.

4. Venue is proper in Berkshire Superior Court as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at 123 Maple Street, Pittsfield, MA 01201.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On July 15, 2023, while walking on the sidewalk in front of 123 Maple Street in Pittsfield, Massachusetts, a large branch of a maple tree on Thomas Reynolds' property fell and struck Jessica Carter, causing her to suffer a shoulder strain and multiple bruises.

6. Prior to the incident, it was known to Thomas Reynolds and neighbors that the branch in question appeared cracked and droopy, posing a risk of falling.

7. Despite being made aware of the hazardous condition of the tree, Thomas Reynolds neglected to take action to remedy the situation and failed to address the dangerous branch.

8. The falling of the tree branch on Jessica Carter was a direct result of Thomas Reynolds' negligence in maintaining the tree and ensuring the safety of pedestrians on the sidewalk.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF

COUNT I: NEGLIGENCE

9. The Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain his property in a safe condition for pedestrians and guests.

10. The Defendant breached that duty by failing to remove the hazardous tree branch, despite having knowledge of its dangerous condition.

11. The breach of duty by the Defendant was the proximate cause of the injuries sustained by the Plaintiff.

12. As a direct and proximate result of the Defendant's negligence, the Plaintiff suffered physical injuries, shoulder strain, multiple bruises, incurred medical expenses of approximately $2,500, and lost wages in the amount of $600.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Carter respectfully requests that this Honorable Court enter judgment in her favor and against the Defendant Thomas Reynolds for:

a. Compensatory damages in the amount of $3,100;
b. Costs and expenses incurred in this action;
c. Any other relief that the Court deems just and proper.
