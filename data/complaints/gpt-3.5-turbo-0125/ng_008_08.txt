COMPLAINT FOR NEGLIGENCE

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Michael Johnson ("Plaintiff"), by and through undersigned counsel, and for his complaint states as follows:

Parties

1. Plaintiff, Michael Johnson, is a resident of Norfolk County, Massachusetts.
2. Defendant, Quincy Bistro, is a business entity located at 123 Main St, Quincy, MA 02169.

Jurisdiction and Venue

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 218, Section 3.
4. Venue is proper in Norfolk County as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at the Defendant's premises located at 123 Main St, Quincy, MA 02169.

Factual Allegations

5. On September 15, 2023, at Quincy Bistro, Plaintiff was a patron having dinner.
6. A server at Quincy Bistro dropped a tray of water glasses in the narrow hallway between the kitchen and dining room.
7. Although the server promptly picked up the broken glass, she left water and ice on the tile floor without placing any warning signs.
8. Approximately ten minutes later, Plaintiff, unaware of the hazardous condition, attempted to walk through the hallway to use the bathroom.
9. Due to the wet and slippery floor, Plaintiff slipped and twisted his knee, resulting in a sprain.
10. Subsequent conversations overheard by Plaintiff indicated that the staff was aware of the hazardous condition and had been "dodging that puddle" prior to the incident.
11. As a result of the fall, Plaintiff sustained injuries requiring medical treatment, including a knee brace and physical therapy.

Count I: Negligence

12. Plaintiff adopts and re-alleges the preceding paragraphs as if fully set forth herein.
13. Quincy Bistro owed a duty of care to Plaintiff to maintain its premises in a safe condition for patrons.
14. Quincy Bistro breached its duty of care by failing to clean up the spilled water promptly and failing to provide any warning of the slippery condition.
15. The breach of duty by Quincy Bistro was the direct and proximate cause of Plaintiff's injuries and resulting damages.
16. As a direct result of Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff suffered physical pain, emotional distress, medical expenses of approximately $2,500, and lost wages in the amount of $1,200.

Prayer for Relief

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Michael Johnson respectfully requests judgment against Defendant Quincy Bistro as follows:
1. Compensatory damages in the amount of $3,700.
2. Costs and fees as allowable by law.
3. Any other relief deemed just and proper by the Court.
