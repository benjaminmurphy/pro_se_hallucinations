III. Factual Allegations

5. On September 30, 2023, at approximately [Time], Plaintiff was lawfully present at the premises of Greenway Grocery Store located at 123 Main St, Somerville, MA.

6. While shopping in the dairy aisle of the store, Plaintiff slipped and fell on a puddle of spilled milk, sustaining a hip sprain as a result of the fall.

7. The puddle of spilled milk was of a sizeable nature, as evidenced by visible cart tracks traversing through it, yet Defendant failed to place any warning signs or cones in the vicinity to alert patrons of the hazardous condition.

8. An employee of Greenway Grocery Store who was stocking shelves in close proximity to the spill acknowledged that he had noticed the spill "a few minutes ago" but neglected to promptly clean it up, causing Plaintiff's injuries.

9. Plaintiff attempted to continue shopping after the fall, but later sought medical attention at an urgent care facility due to increasing pain in her hip which resulted in a diagnosis of a hip sprain and necessitated subsequent physical therapy.

10. As a direct result of the Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff incurred medical expenses totaling approximately $1,200 and suffered lost wages amounting to approximately $800 due to missed work opportunities at her place of employment.

IV. Causes of Action

11. The Defendant breached its duty of care owed to the Plaintiff by failing to maintain a safe premise and eliminate known hazards, thereby committing negligence.
