FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On or about October 1, 2023, the Plaintiff Jessica Mills was a customer at the Defendant's store located at 123 Market St, Lowell, MA 01852.

6. The Defendant had heavy boxes of appliances stacked without any safety measures in place, such as straps or barriers, at a height of approximately five to six boxes high.

7. Another customer negligently bumped into a shelf, causing a large toaster-oven box located near the top to fall and strike the Plaintiff on her shoulder and head.

8. As a result of the incident, the Plaintiff immediately experienced dizziness and a severe headache. Subsequently, she sought medical treatment at the emergency room where she was diagnosed with a mild concussion and a shoulder strain.

9. The Plaintiff has incurred approximately $1,200 in medical expenses and has lost approximately $600 in wages due to the injuries sustained as a direct result of the Defendant's negligence.

CAUSE OF ACTION - NEGLIGENCE

10. The Defendant had a duty to exercise reasonable care to maintain a safe shopping environment for its customers.

11. The Defendant breached its duty of care by failing to properly secure the stacked heavy boxes of appliances, creating a hazardous condition that ultimately led to the Plaintiff's injuries.

12. The Defendant's negligence was the direct and proximate cause of the Plaintiff's mild concussion and shoulder strain.

13. As a result of the Defendant's negligence, the Plaintiff suffered physical injuries, medical expenses, and lost wages.
