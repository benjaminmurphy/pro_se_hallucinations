FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On August 15, 2023, at the premises of Home Goods Warehouse, Plaintiff was examining kitchen appliances when a large box fell from a shelf and struck Plaintiff on the shoulder and head, causing injuries.

6. The box that fell was one of several stacked on a high shelf without proper safety measures such as straps or barriers to prevent them from falling.

7. The incident was caused by the negligent actions of another customer who bumped into a shelf, dislodging the box that struck Plaintiff.

8. Defendant's employees were aware of the dangerous condition of the shelves but failed to take adequate precautions or provide warnings to protect customers.

CAUSES OF ACTION

9. Plaintiff alleges that Defendant's negligence directly caused the injuries suffered by Plaintiff. The following causes of action are brought in support of this claim:

   A. Negligence: Defendant owed a duty of care to Plaintiff to maintain safe premises. By failing to secure stacked boxes and provide a warning of the foreseeable danger, Defendant breached this duty and directly caused Plaintiff's injuries. The injuries suffered by Plaintiff were a foreseeable consequence of Defendant's negligence.
