FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On May 1, 2022, Plaintiff entered into a lease agreement with Defendant to rent the property located at 123 Maple Street, Lowell, MA 01851.

6. Plaintiff has been paying monthly rent of $1200 to Defendant as per the terms of the lease.

7. Plaintiff discovered the presence of mold in the bathroom of the property, starting in the shower corner and spreading to the ceiling.

8. Despite Plaintiff's complaints, Defendant failed to remediate the mold and blamed Plaintiff for the issue based on unsubstantiated claims regarding Plaintiff's showering habits.

11. Defendant has refused to address the ventilation issue in the bathroom, asserting that Plaintiff is responsible for the mold problem.

CAUSE OF ACTION

12. Plaintiff asserts a single cause of action for breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment against Defendant.

13. Defendant's failure to address and remediate the mold infestation in the property's bathroom, despite being notified and its detrimental effects on Plaintiff's health, constitutes a breach of the implied covenant of quiet enjoyment.
