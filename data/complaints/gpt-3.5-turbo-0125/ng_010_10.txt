COMPLAINT FOR NEGLIGENCE

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Jessica Hart ("Plaintiff"), by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant Mike Thompson ("Defendant"), states as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Jessica Hart, is an individual residing at [Your Address], [City, State, Zip].

2. Defendant, Mike Thompson, is an individual believed to reside at 45 Maple Avenue, Pittsfield, Massachusetts.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises out of a personal injury sustained by Plaintiff due to Defendant's negligence. The Berkshire Superior Court has jurisdiction over this matter as the incident giving rise to this action occurred within Berkshire County, Massachusetts.

4. Venue is proper in Berkshire Superior Court as the incident took place at 45 Maple Avenue, Pittsfield, Massachusetts.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On July 15, 2023, at the premises located at 45 Maple Avenue, Pittsfield, Massachusetts, Defendant, Mike Thompson, owned, controlled, and maintained a property with a large maple tree having a cracked and droopy branch overhanging the sidewalk.

6. Multiple neighbors and pedestrians, including Plaintiff, had brought to Defendant's attention the hazardous condition of the cracked branch and advised him to seek professional assessment and necessary remedial action.

7. Despite being aware of the dangerous condition of the tree branch, Defendant neglected to address the issue promptly and failed to engage an arborist or take any measures to ensure the safety of individuals walking on the sidewalk.

8. On July 15, 2023, during a routine thunderstorm, the cracked branch of the tree, through Defendant's negligence and inaction, detached and fell onto Plaintiff as she was passing by, causing her to suffer a shoulder strain and multiple bruises, requiring medical treatment and resulting in financial losses.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF

9. Plaintiff incorporates the foregoing paragraphs as if fully set forth herein.

10. Defendant owed a duty of care to Plaintiff to maintain the tree on his property in a safe condition and to prevent foreseeable harm to individuals using the sidewalk.

11. Defendant breached this duty by failing to address the known hazardous condition of the tree promptly, despite receiving notice and warnings from concerned individuals.

12. As a direct and proximate result of Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff suffered a shoulder strain, multiple bruises, incurred medical expenses of approximately $2,500, and lost wages amounting to $600.

13. Plaintiff seeks damages for pain and suffering, medical expenses, lost wages, and any other relief this Court deems just and proper.

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Hart respectfully requests this Honorable Court to:

(1) Award compensatory damages in an amount to be determined at trial but not less than $3,100;
(2) Award pre-and post-judgment interest on the damages awarded;
(3) Grant such other and further relief as the Court deems just and proper; and
(4) Provide any and all other relief to which Plaintiff may be entitled.
