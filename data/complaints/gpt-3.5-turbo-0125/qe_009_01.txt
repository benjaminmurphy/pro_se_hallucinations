COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Jessica Li, by and through the undersigned, and for her Complaint against Defendant Thomas Reed, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Jessica Li is a resident of 123 Maple Street, Springfield, MA.

2. Defendant Thomas Reed is the landlord of the aforementioned property.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over the subject matter of this action pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 218, Section 21, as this is a civil action arising under Massachusetts landlord-tenant law.

4. Venue is proper in this district as the property in question is located within the jurisdiction of the Springfield District Court.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On January 15, 2022, Plaintiff entered into a residential lease agreement with Defendant for an apartment located at 123 Maple Street, Springfield, MA.

6. Since approximately October 20, 2025, Plaintiff has been deprived of electricity in one of the bedrooms of the leased premises due to faulty wiring.

7. Despite Plaintiff's repeated complaints, Defendant has failed to rectify the electrical issue, causing Plaintiff substantial inconvenience and financial loss.

8. Plaintiff has been forced to incur additional expenses by working from alternative locations, such as coffee shops, and by renting a coworking space membership, totaling approximately $450 in damages.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF

9. Defendant has breached the covenant of quiet enjoyment as defined under Massachusetts law, by failing to maintain habitable premises with the necessary amenities, specifically electricity in one of the bedrooms, thereby disrupting Plaintiff's use and enjoyment of the leased property.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Li respectfully requests that this Court grant the following relief:

1. Monetary damages in the amount of $450.

2. Such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.
