COMPLAINT

Plaintiff, Michael Thompson, by and through undersigned counsel, for his Complaint against HomeGoods Discount Store, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Michael Thompson, is a resident of Lowell, Middlesex County, Massachusetts.

2. Defendant, HomeGoods Discount Store, is a business entity operating in the State of Massachusetts, with a place of business at 123 Main St, Lowell, MA 01851.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 223, Section 9, as the amount in controversy exceeds the minimum jurisdictional limit of this Court.

4. Venue is proper in Middlesex Superior Court, Lowell Division, as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at the aforementioned HomeGoods store location.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 15, 2023, at the HomeGoods Discount Store located at 123 Main St, Lowell, MA 01851, Plaintiff Michael Thompson was browsing the kitchen appliance section.

6. Defendant had stacked heavy boxes of appliances on high shelves without sufficient securing measures such as straps or barriers.

7. Another customer's cart collided with one of the lower shelves, causing a heavy toaster-oven box to fall several feet and strike Plaintiff on the shoulder and side of the head.

8. As a result of the incident, Plaintiff immediately suffered dizziness and a severe headache, prompting the store manager to fill out an incident report.

9. Despite the initial offer of a store coupon, Plaintiff's symptoms worsened that night, leading him to seek medical attention at the emergency room.

10. Medical evaluation revealed that Plaintiff had sustained a mild concussion and shoulder strain, necessitating additional treatment and causing him to miss work as a delivery driver and abstain from sports activities.

CAUSES OF ACTION

11. Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain a safe shopping environment for its customers, including securing stacked merchandise to prevent foreseeable harm.

12. Defendant breached this duty by negligently stacking heavy items without adequate precautions, directly causing Plaintiff's injuries.

13. Such negligence constitutes a breach of the duty owed by Defendant to Plaintiff under Massachusetts negligence law.

REQUEST FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Michael Thompson respectfully prays for judgment against Defendant HomeGoods Discount Store as follows:

a. Compensatory damages in an amount to be determined at trial but not less than $2,000, covering medical expenses, lost wages, and pain and suffering;

b. Prejudgment and postjudgment interest as allowed by law;

c. Costs and attorneys' fees incurred in pursuing this action; and

d. Any further relief as the Court deems just and proper.
