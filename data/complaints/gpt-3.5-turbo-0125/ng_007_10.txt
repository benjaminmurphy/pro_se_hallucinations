COMPLAINT

NOW COMES the Plaintiff, James Miller, by and through undersigned counsel, and files this Complaint against the Defendant, Brockton Community Center, and alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. The Plaintiff, James Miller, is a resident of Brockton, Massachusetts.

2. The Defendant, Brockton Community Center, is a public entity located at 1234 Main St, Brockton, MA 02301.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 218, Section 21.

4. Venue is proper in the Brockton District Court as the incident in question occurred at the Defendant's facility located in Brockton.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 15, 2023, at the Brockton Community Center, the Plaintiff's son, a participant in the after-school program, was injured due to the negligence of the Defendant's staff members.

6. Despite advertising continuous supervision, the Defendant's staff failed to adequately monitor the children present in the gym.

7. Specifically, on the day in question, a staff member left the gym area to attend to administrative tasks, leaving one staff member in charge who was distracted by personal phone use.

8. Two older boys engaged in a rough tackle game on the gym's hardwood floor, despite complaints from other children present.

9. The staff member failed to intervene or control the situation, stating "boys will be boys," which resulted in the Plaintiff's eight-year-old son being violently tackled by one of the older boys, causing him to suffer a concussion.

CAUSES OF ACTION

10. The Defendant owed a duty of care to the Plaintiff's son to provide adequate supervision and ensure a safe environment during the after-school program at the community center.

11. The Defendant breached its duty of care by failing to monitor the children, failing to address the dangerous behavior of the older boys, and allowing rough play that led to the Plaintiff's son suffering a concussion.

12. The Defendant's negligence directly caused the Plaintiff's son's injuries and resulting damages.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court enter judgment in his favor against the Defendant, Brockton Community Center, and award the following relief:

13. Compensatory damages in the amount of $5,000 for medical expenses.
14. Compensatory damages in the amount of $1,200 for lost wages.
15. Any further relief that this Court deems just and proper.
