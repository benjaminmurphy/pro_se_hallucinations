COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jessica Turner, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against the Defendant, Maria Johnson, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Jessica Turner, is an individual residing at 81 Maple Street, Lowell, MA 01852.
2. Defendant, Maria Johnson, is the landlord of the aforementioned property.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter as it involves a landlord-tenant dispute arising in the city of Lowell, MA.
4. Venue is proper in the Lesser District Court of Lowell as the subject property is located within its jurisdiction.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. Plaintiff entered into a residential lease agreement with Defendant for the rental of the property located at 81 Maple Street, Lowell, MA 01852, commencing on June 1, 2021.
6. Plaintiff pays monthly rent of $1,200 to Defendant.
7. Plaintiff discovered the presence of mold in her bathroom, which originated in the shower corner and spread to the ceiling. Plaintiff promptly informed Defendant of the mold issue and its concerning extent.
8. Defendant inspected the bathroom and placed blame on Plaintiff, alleging that Plaintiff's shower habits were the cause of the mold growth due to inadequate ventilation.
9. Plaintiff disputes Defendant's allegations, asserting that she runs the exhaust fan during showers, although not exclusively, and that her shower routine is not abnormal.
10. Plaintiff has suffered health consequences since the mold appeared, experiencing respiratory issues such as coughing, congestion, and wheezing, as diagnosed by her medical provider, attributing these symptoms to the mold exposure.
11. Plaintiff has incurred damages in the approximate amount of $60 due to the disposal of mold-affected items, including a shower curtain and towels.
12. Defendant has refused to remedy the ventilation issue in the bathroom, attributing fault to Plaintiff for the mold problem.

CAUSE OF ACTION

13. Plaintiff alleges a breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment by Defendant due to the mold-infested living conditions in the leased premises, which has adversely impacted Plaintiff's health and enjoyment of the property.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Turner respectfully requests that this Court:

a. Enter judgment in favor of Plaintiff on the claim of breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment;
b. Award damages in the amount of $600, plus court costs and attorney fees;
c. Order Defendant to remediate the mold issue and ensure proper ventilation in the bathroom; and
d. Grant any other relief that the Court deems just and proper.
