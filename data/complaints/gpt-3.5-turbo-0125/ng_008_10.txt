FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On August 15, 2023, at approximately [time], the Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson, was having dinner at the Defendant's establishment, Quincy Bistro LLC, located at 125 Washington Street, Quincy, MA.

6. While at the restaurant, a server of the Defendant dropped a tray with several water glasses in the narrow hallway between the kitchen and the dining room.

7. The server failed to adequately clean up the spilled water and ice, and did not place any warning signs or barriers to indicate the hazardous condition.

8. Approximately ten minutes after the spill, the Plaintiff got up from her table to use the bathroom and inadvertently stepped on the wet floor, causing her to slip and twist her knee.

9. Following the incident, it was discovered that various staff members had been aware of the hazardous condition and had been "dodging" the puddle in the hallway.

10. As a result of the slip and fall incident, the Plaintiff suffered a sprained knee which required medical treatment, a knee brace, and physical therapy.

CAUSES OF ACTION

11. The Plaintiff incorporates by reference the allegations set forth in paragraphs 1 through 10.

12. The Defendant, Quincy Bistro LLC, owed a duty of care to maintain a safe environment for its patrons and guests.

13. The Defendant breached its duty of care by failing to promptly clean up the hazardous condition caused by the spilled water and ice, and by failing to warn patrons of the dangerous situation.

14. The Defendant's negligence in maintaining its premises directly and proximately caused the Plaintiff's injuries.

15. As a direct and proximate result of the Defendant's negligence, the Plaintiff sustained injuries, incurred medical expenses, and suffered loss of wages.

b. Prejudgment and post-judgment interest as allowed by law;
c. Costs and reasonable attorneys' fees incurred in this action; and
d. Any other relief that this Court deems just and proper.
