COMPLAINT

Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson, by and through undersigned counsel, brings this action against Defendant, Michael Anderson, and alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Jessica Thompson, is an individual residing at 123 Elm Street, Somerville, MA.

2. Defendant, Michael Anderson, is the landlord of the aforementioned property located at 123 Elm Street, Somerville, MA.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 218, Section 19.

4. Venue is proper in the District Court for the County of Somerville as the subject property is located within its jurisdiction.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On January 15, 2022, Plaintiff entered into a lease agreement with Defendant for the rental of an apartment at 123 Elm Street, Somerville, MA.

6. The lease agreement includes a covenant of quiet enjoyment, guaranteeing Plaintiff the right to peaceful and quiet habitation of the leased premises.

7. Since the new tenants moved into the unit above Plaintiff six months ago, they have engaged in continuous disruptive behavior, including but not limited to loud stomping, late-night music, and frequent furniture moving.

8. Plaintiff has repeatedly requested the noisy tenants to lower the volume, with only temporary compliance.

9. Plaintiff has lodged multiple complaints with Defendant regarding the disruptive behavior of the upstairs tenants but has received inadequate response.

10. Defendant, Michael Anderson, failed to take reasonable steps to remedy the noise issues, stating that he "can't control how people live."

11. The continuous noise disturbances have resulted in Plaintiff experiencing severe sleep deprivation, leading to negative consequences at work and requiring medical intervention with associated costs.

CAUSE OF ACTION

12. Plaintiff asserts a claim for breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment against Defendant, Michael Anderson, based on Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 186, Section 14, which guarantees tenants the right to quiet enjoyment of their rented premises.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court:

A. Declare that Defendant has breached the covenant of quiet enjoyment as guaranteed under Massachusetts law;

B. Award Plaintiff monetary damages in the amount of $2000 for the mental anguish, medical expenses, and inconvenience caused by the breach;

C. Grant any further relief that the Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff hereby demands a jury trial on all claims so triable.
