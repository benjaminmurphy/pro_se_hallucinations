COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Jessica Martinez, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against the Defendant Robert Hargrove, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Jessica Martinez is an individual residing at 123 Elm Street, Brockton, MA 02301.
2. Defendant Robert Hargrove is the landlord of the aforementioned property.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 218, Section 21.
4. Venue is proper in this district as the subject property is located within the jurisdiction of this Court.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. Plaintiff entered into a residential lease agreement with the Defendant for the property located at 123 Elm Street, Brockton, MA on April 1, 2022.
6. On or about [Date], Plaintiff reported to Defendant that the hot water in her apartment had ceased functioning.
7. Defendant acknowledged the issue and informed Plaintiff that a part needed to be replaced in the water heater.
8. Despite Defendant's assurances that the part was ordered, subsequent delays in repairs occurred.
9. As of the filing of this Complaint, approximately three weeks later, Plaintiff continues to be deprived of hot water essential for basic living needs and personal hygiene.
10. Plaintiff has been forced to incur additional expenses totaling approximately $30 per month for gym showers, as well as the cost of transportation, in the absence of proper hot water facilities.
11. Moreover, Plaintiff has had to bear the burden of taking her baby to her mother's residence for bathing, incurring additional inconvenience and cost.
12. Defendant's failure to expedite the repair of the hot water system in a timely manner has caused substantial inconvenience and financial hardship to Plaintiff.

CAUSE OF ACTION 
(For Breach of Covenant of Quiet Enjoyment)

13. Defendant's failure to provide and maintain hot water as required by the lease agreement constitutes a breach of the implied covenant of quiet enjoyment.
14. The breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment gives rise to a cause of action under Massachusetts law, entitling Plaintiff to seek appropriate relief.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Martinez respectfully requests that this Court:

15. Enter judgment in favor of Plaintiff for compensatory damages in the amount of $450.
16. Grant such other and further relief as this Court deems just and proper.
