FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

4. On February 1, 2023, the Plaintiff entered into a lease agreement with the Defendant to rent a studio apartment located at 567 Jamaica Plain Ave, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130.

5. During the showing of the apartment, the Defendant assured the Plaintiff that water stains on the ceiling were from a previous leak that had been "totally fixed.‚Äù

6. Despite this assurance, upon the occurrence of the first significant rainstorm, water began dripping through the same spot on the ceiling.

7. The Plaintiff promptly notified the Defendant of the ongoing issue, which the Defendant dismissively referred to as a "minor cosmetic issue."

8. Subsequent rainstorms exacerbated the leakage, causing damage to the Plaintiff's personal property, including her couch and laptop.

11. The Defendant's failure to properly address and rectify the water leakage issue constitutes a breach of the implied warranty of habitability under Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 186, Section 14.
