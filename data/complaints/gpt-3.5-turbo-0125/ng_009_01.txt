COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jane Doe, and for her Complaint against the Defendant, ABC Realty Management, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. The Plaintiff, Jane Doe, is an individual residing at 123 Maple St, Lynn, MA.

2. The Defendant, ABC Realty Management, is a company responsible for the management of the property located at the same address.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises under the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Jurisdiction is proper in this Court pursuant to [Massachusetts General Laws Chapter XX, Section XX, etc.].

4. Venue is proper in the Lynn District Court as the events giving rise to this action occurred at 123 Maple St, Lynn, MA.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On or about September 15, 2023, a fire broke out in a first-floor apartment at 123 Maple St, Lynn, MA, due to kitchen-related issues.

6. The Defendant, ABC Realty Management, was responsible for the maintenance of smoke detectors in the common hallways of the apartment building, including the smoke detector located outside the Plaintiff's unit.

7. The Plaintiff repeatedly notified the Defendant that the smoke detector outside her unit was chirping due to a low battery. Despite assurances from the Defendant, the issue persisted without resolution.

8. As a result of the malfunctioning smoke detector, when the fire broke out, the hallway detector failed to sound an alarm, depriving the Plaintiff of timely warning.

9. The Plaintiff suffered smoke inhalation and difficulty breathing while attempting to evacuate the building. She was subsequently hospitalized and incurred medical expenses of approximately $7,500. Additionally, the Plaintiff lost $2,000 in wages due to the incident.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF

10. The Defendant owed a duty to the Plaintiff to maintain and ensure the proper functioning of the smoke detectors in the common areas of the apartment building.

11. The Defendant breached its duty by failing to promptly address the malfunctioning smoke detector outside the Plaintiff's unit, leading to the Plaintiff's injuries and damages.

12. The Defendant's negligence directly caused the Plaintiff's injuries, medical expenses, and lost wages.

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff Jane Doe respectfully requests judgment against the Defendant ABC Realty Management for:

a. Compensatory damages in the amount of $9,500;
b. Costs and reasonable attorneys' fees;
c. Such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.
