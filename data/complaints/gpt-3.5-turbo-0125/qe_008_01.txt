COMPLAINT

1. Plaintiff Emily Thompson ("Plaintiff") brings this action against Defendant John Ramirez ("Defendant") and alleges as follows:

PARTIES

2. Plaintiff, Emily Thompson, is an individual residing at 45 Broadway St, Chelsea, MA 02150.

3. Defendant, John Ramirez, is the landlord of the property where Plaintiff resides.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

4. This Court has jurisdiction over this action pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 218, Section 21.

5. Venue is proper in Chelsea District Court as the subject property is located within the jurisdiction of this Court.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

6. Plaintiff entered into a rental agreement with Defendant for the apartment at 45 Broadway St, Chelsea, MA 02150, commencing on January 15, 2022.

7. Despite repeated notifications to Defendant regarding a broken lock on the main entry door of the building, Defendant has failed to remedy the issue for approximately two months.

8. Due to the broken lock, unauthorized individuals gained access to the building resulting in theft of mail, including a package belonging to Plaintiff valued at $120.

9. Additionally, Plaintiff has discovered evidence of an unauthorized individual occupying the basement of the building, causing distress and insecurity.

10. In response to the security concerns caused by Defendant's inaction, Plaintiff incurred expenses amounting to approximately $200 for the purchase of a security camera and a better deadbolt.

CAUSE OF ACTION

11. Defendant's failure to address the broken lock, resulting in breaches of security, unauthorized access to the building, theft, and unauthorized occupancy of the basement, constitutes a violation of the covenant of quiet enjoyment as established under Massachusetts common law.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court:

a. Compensate Plaintiff in the amount of $320 for damages incurred;
b. Order Defendant to promptly repair the broken lock and take necessary security measures to ensure Plaintiff's quiet enjoyment of the premises;
c. Award any further relief that the Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff demands a trial by jury on all triable issues.
