COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Sarah Thompson, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant Linda Martinez, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Sarah Thompson is a tenant residing at 123 Maple Street, Lowell, MA 01851.

2. Defendant Linda Martinez is the landlord of the premises located at [Landlord's Address], Lowell, MA 01851.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 218, Section 21 and Chapter 185C, Section 3.

4. Venue is proper in the Lowell District Court as the subject property is located within its jurisdiction.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On or around [Date], Plaintiff discovered mold growth in the bathroom of the rented premises located at 123 Maple Street, Lowell, MA 01851.

6. Plaintiff duly notified Defendant of the mold issue and requested remediation.

7. Defendant negligently attributed the presence of mold to Plaintiff, claiming inadequate ventilation practices and hot showers as the cause, despite the absence of proper ventilation in the bathroom.

8. The mold condition has continued to worsen, spreading from the shower corner to the ceiling, leading to adverse health effects on Plaintiff, including respiratory issues such as coughing, congestion, and wheezing.

9. Plaintiff consulted a medical professional who attributed her health issues to the mold exposure, necessitating remediation of the premises.

10. As a result of the mold infestation, Plaintiff has incurred damages in the form of discarded personal property, including a shower curtain and towels valued at approximately $60.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF

11. Plaintiff alleges that Defendant's failure to address and remediate the mold infestation constitutes a breach of the implied warranty of habitability and the covenant of quiet enjoyment under Massachusetts law.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Sarah Thompson respectfully requests that this Court enter judgment in her favor and against Defendant Linda Martinez as follows:

a. Ordering Defendant to remediate the mold infestation in the rented premises;
b. Awarding Plaintiff monetary damages in the amount of $500 for property loss and health-related expenses;
c. Granting such other and further relief as the Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff demands a trial by jury on all triable issues.
