JURISDICTION

3. This action arises out of a landlord-tenant relationship and addresses violations of the covenant of quiet enjoyment under Massachusetts law. Jurisdiction is proper in the Boston Housing Court pursuant to Mass. Gen. Laws ch. 186, ยง 14 and Mass. Gen. Laws ch. 185C, ยง 16.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

4. On February 1, 2023, Plaintiff entered into a lease agreement with Defendant to rent a studio apartment located at 67 Forest Hills Street, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130.

5. During the showing of the apartment, Plaintiff raised concerns regarding water stains on the ceiling, to which Defendant assured Plaintiff that the issue had been rectified.

6. Shortly after moving in, Plaintiff experienced water leakage and subsequent damages during rainstorms, consistent with the prior issues Plaintiff had observed.

7. Plaintiff has made multiple complaints to Defendant regarding the ongoing water leakage issue, but Defendant has negligently and willfully failed to address and remedy the problem adequately.

8. The water leakage has caused damage to Plaintiff's personal property, including a laptop costing approximately $900, as well as creating a musty odor and potential health hazard from mold growth.

CAUSE OF ACTION

9. Plaintiff alleges that Defendant's failure to adequately address and remedy the water leakage issue constitutes a breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment under Mass. Gen. Laws ch. 186, ยง 14.
