COMPLAINT FOR NEGLIGENCE

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Jessica Hall, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant HomeGoods Plus, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Jessica Hall is a resident of Lowell, Massachusetts.

2. Defendant HomeGoods Plus is a discount home-goods store located at 1230 Main St, Lowell, MA 01851.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws chapter 218, section 19, as the amount in controversy does not exceed $25,000.

4. Venue is proper in the Lowell District Court as the incident giving rise to this action occurred within its jurisdiction.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 20, 2023, at the premises of 1230 Main St, Lowell, MA 01851, Plaintiff Jessica Hall was browsing the kitchen aisle in HomeGoods Plus.

6. Defendant HomeGoods Plus had negligently stacked heavy boxes of appliances several boxes high without proper securing devices or barriers to prevent them from falling.

7. Another customer pushing a cart down the aisle made contact with a shelf, causing a large toaster-oven box to fall from near the top shelf, striking Plaintiff on her shoulder and head.

8. As a result of the falling box, Plaintiff suffered a mild concussion and shoulder strain, leading to medical expenses of approximately $2,500 and lost wages of approximately $1,200.

CAUSES OF ACTION

9. Defendant HomeGoods Plus owed a duty of care to Plaintiff to maintain a safe environment for customers.

10. Defendant breached this duty by failing to properly secure stacked boxes, thereby creating an unreasonable risk of harm.

11. Defendant's breach of duty directly caused Plaintiff's injuries.

12. Defendant's negligence forms the basis of this action.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Hall respectfully requests that this Court enter judgment in her favor and against Defendant HomeGoods Plus for:

(a) Compensatory damages in the amount of $3,700;
(b) Costs and disbursements incurred in prosecuting this action;
(c) Such further relief as the Court deems just and proper.
