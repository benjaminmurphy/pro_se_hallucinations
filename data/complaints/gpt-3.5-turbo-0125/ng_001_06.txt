COMPLAINT FOR NEGLIGENCE

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Emily Carter, by and through undersigned counsel, and complains against Defendant Somerville Grocery Store LLC as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Emily Carter is a resident of Massachusetts, with an address at [Your Address].

2. Defendant Somerville Grocery Store LLC is a business entity registered and operating in Massachusetts, with a place of business at 150 Washington St, Somerville, MA 02143.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter as the amount in controversy exceeds the minimum threshold required for the Superior Court.

4. Venue is proper in the Middlesex Superior Court, Civil Division, as the cause of action arose in Somerville, within the county of Middlesex.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On August 12, 2023, Plaintiff was shopping at the Somerville Grocery Store located at 150 Washington St, Somerville, MA 02143.

6. While in the dairy aisle of the store, Plaintiff stepped into a large puddle of spilled milk, causing her to slip and fall, resulting in a hip sprain.

7. The puddle of spilled milk was substantial in size, noticeable, and had cart tracks going through it, indicating it had been present for a significant period of time.

8. Despite the obvious hazard, Defendant failed to place any warning signs or cones alerting customers to the dangerous condition.

9. An employee of the store, positioned in close proximity to the spill, admitted to having noticed the spill several minutes before the incident but neglected to address it promptly.

10. Plaintiff, experiencing pain that evening, sought medical attention the following day, where she was diagnosed with a hip sprain requiring physical therapy.

CAUSES OF ACTION

11. Defendant owed a duty of care to Plaintiff as a business invitee to maintain safe premises and to promptly address known hazards.

12. Defendant breached this duty by failing to timely clean up the spilled milk and failing to warn Plaintiff of the dangerous condition.

13. Defendant's negligence directly caused Plaintiff's injuries and resulting damages.

CLAIM FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Emily Carter respectfully requests judgment against Defendant Somerville Grocery Store LLC for the following relief:

a. Compensatory damages in the amount of $3,700, representing medical expenses of $2,500 and lost wages of $1,200;
b. Pre-judgment and post-judgment interest as allowed by law;
c. Costs and fees incurred in prosecuting this action; and
d. Any other relief this Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff demands a trial by jury on all issues so triable.
