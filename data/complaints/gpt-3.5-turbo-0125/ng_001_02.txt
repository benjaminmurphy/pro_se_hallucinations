COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Emily Johnson, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against the Defendant, Whole Foods Market, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Emily Johnson, is a resident of Somerville, Massachusetts.

2. Defendant, Whole Foods Market, is a corporation authorized to do business in Massachusetts and operates a grocery store at 45 Beacon Street, Somerville, MA 02143.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises under the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to M.G.L. c. 218, § 19.

4. Venue is proper in Somerville District Court as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at the aforementioned Whole Foods Market location in Somerville, MA 02143.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 16, 2023, Plaintiff Emily Johnson was shopping at the Whole Foods Market located at 45 Beacon Street, Somerville, MA 02143.

6. While in the dairy aisle, Plaintiff stepped into a large puddle of spilled milk, which was noticeable by the presence of cart tracks running through it. There were no warning signs or cones alerting customers to the hazardous condition.

7. An employee of the Defendant was stocking shelves approximately fifteen feet away from the spill and admitted to being aware of the spill "a few minutes ago" but neglected to take immediate action to clean it up.

8. As a result of slipping on the spilled milk, Plaintiff suffered a hip sprain that required medical attention and physical therapy.

9. Plaintiff incurred approximately $2,000 in medical expenses and lost $1,500 in wages due to missed work shifts at her job.

CAUSE OF ACTION

10. The Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain a safe environment for its customers, which includes promptly addressing and cleaning up spills to prevent foreseeable harm.

11. The Defendant breached its duty of care by failing to promptly clean up the spilled milk, provide warning signs, or take reasonable precautions to prevent the hazardous condition.

12. The Defendant's breach of duty was a proximate cause of Plaintiff's injuries and resulting damages.

13. Plaintiff brings forth a cause of action against the Defendant for negligence under Massachusetts law.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Emily Johnson respectfully requests that this Honorable Court enter judgment in her favor and against the Defendant, Whole Foods Market, for the following relief:

a. Compensatory damages in an amount to be determined at trial but not less than $3,500;
b. Prejudgment and post-judgment interest as allowed by law;
c. Costs and expenses of this action; and
d. Any other relief that the Court deems just and proper.
