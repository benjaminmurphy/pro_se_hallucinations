Complaint for Modification

Now comes the Plaintiff, Emily Johnson, and for her Complaint for Modification against the Defendant, Michael Johnson, states as follows:

1. The Parties
1.1. The Plaintiff, Emily Johnson, is a resident of [Your City], Massachusetts.
1.2. The Defendant, Michael Johnson, is a resident of Cape Cod, Massachusetts.

2. Relevant Background
2.1. On May 15, 2019, a custody order was entered by this Honorable Court, granting primary custody of the minor child, Sophie Johnson (age 8), to the Plaintiff with visitation rights awarded to the Defendant.
2.2. Pursuant to the existing custody order, the Defendant is entitled to visitation every other weekend and one midweek overnight on Wednesdays.
2.3. Since the entry of the custody order, the Defendant has relocated to Cape Cod, approximately ninety miles away from the Plaintiff's residence.

3. Material Change in Circumstances
3.1. The Plaintiff avers that there has been a material change in circumstances warranting modification of the existing custody order due to the Defendant's relocation to Cape Cod.
3.2. The current arrangement, specifically the midweek overnight on Wednesdays, has become impractical and detrimental to the well-being of the minor child.

4. Allegations
4.1. The Defendant's relocation to Cape Cod has significantly increased the travel time and distance associated with the midweek overnight visitation.
4.2. The minor child, Sophie Johnson, has been subject to excessive travel on school nights, leading to fatigue and negative impacts on her academic performance.
4.3. Sophie's teacher has observed a noticeable decline in her participation levels on Thursdays following the midweek overnight visitation due to fatigue.

5. Requested Modification
5.1. The Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court modify the existing custody order to eliminate the midweek overnight visitation on Wednesdays and adjust the visitation schedule to better accommodate the minor child's well-being.

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff respectfully requests this Honorable Court to grant the following relief:
1. Modify the existing custody order to eliminate the midweek overnight visitation on Wednesdays.
2. Adjust the visitation schedule in the best interests of the minor child, Sophie Johnson.
3. Grant such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.
