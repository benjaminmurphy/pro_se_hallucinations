COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jacob Miller, by and through the undersigned legal assistant, and for his Complaint against the Defendant, Emily Johnson, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Jacob Miller, is an individual residing at [Plaintiff's Address], Worcester, MA.

2. Defendant, Emily Johnson, is the owner of the property located at 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA, where the incident giving rise to this action occurred.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter as the facts giving rise to the cause of action occurred within Worcester County.

4. Venue is proper in Worcester District Court as the property in question is located in Worcester, MA.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On February 10, 2023, at the property located at 123 Maple Street, Worcester, MA, owned by the Defendant, there was a dangerous ice patch on the sidewalk resulting from the Defendant's negligent shoveling and failure to apply salt or sand.

6. The hazardous ice patch created by the Defendant's actions caused Plaintiff to slip and fall while delivering groceries to a tenant at the mentioned property.

7. Despite being informed by the tenants of the hazardous condition via text and photographic evidence, the Defendant callously disregarded the safety concerns by responding, "the sun will take care of it."

8. As a direct result of the Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff suffered a lower back injury and incurred approximately $2,500 in medical expenses and lost $1,000 in wages due to missed work.

CAUSES OF ACTION

9. Plaintiff brings forth this action against the Defendant, Emily Johnson, for negligence under Massachusetts law.

10. Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain the sidewalk of her property in a reasonably safe condition for lawful visitors.

11. Defendant breached her duty of care by negligently shoveling the sidewalk, failing to apply necessary de-icing materials, and disregarding warnings about the hazardous condition.

12. Defendant's breach of duty was the proximate cause of Plaintiff's injuries and resulting damages.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff prays for judgment against the Defendant, Emily Johnson, as follows:

a. Compensatory damages in the amount of $3,500 for medical expenses and lost wages;

b. Prejudgment and post-judgment interest as allowed by law;

c. Costs and reasonable attorneys' fees; and

d. Any other relief that this Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff demands a trial by jury on all issues so triable.
