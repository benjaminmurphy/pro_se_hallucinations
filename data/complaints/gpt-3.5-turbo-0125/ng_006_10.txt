11. Defendant's negligent conduct in opening his car door into the bike lane without proper regard for the safety of bicyclists constitutes a breach of the duty of care owed to Plaintiff.

12. Plaintiff Mark Johnson asserts a claim for negligence against Defendant Ryan Smith, in accordance with Massachusetts negligence law.
