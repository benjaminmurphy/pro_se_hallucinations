COMPLAINT FOR NEGLIGENCE

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Emily Johnson, is a resident of [Your City], Massachusetts.

2. Defendant, Fresh Foods Grocery, is a corporation registered in the state of Massachusetts and operates a grocery store located at 123 Elm St, Somerville, MA 02144.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Honorable Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 218, Section 3.

4. Venue is proper in the Middlesex Superior Court as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at the aforementioned Fresh Foods Grocery in Somerville, Middlesex County, Massachusetts.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 16, 2023, at approximately [Time], Emily Johnson was shopping at Fresh Foods Grocery when she slipped and fell in a puddle of spilled milk in the dairy aisle.

6. The spill of milk was substantial, with visible cart tracks indicating it had been present for some time prior to the incident.

7. Despite the obvious hazard posed by the spill, there were no warning signs or cones placed to alert customers to the danger.

8. An employee of Fresh Foods Grocery was stocking shelves nearby and admitted to being aware of the spill "a few minutes ago" but failed to take immediate action to clean it up.

9. As a result of the fall, Emily Johnson suffered a hip sprain that required medical treatment, including physical therapy.

CAUSES OF ACTION

10. Plaintiff realleges and incorporates by reference the preceding paragraphs as if fully set forth herein.

11. Defendant owed a duty of care to maintain its premises in a safe condition for its customers.

12. Defendant negligently breached its duty of care by failing to promptly clean up the spilled milk and failing to warn customers of the hazardous condition.

13. The negligence of the Defendant was the direct and proximate cause of the Plaintiff's injuries.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court:

a. Enter judgment in favor of the Plaintiff, Emily Johnson, and against the Defendant, Fresh Foods Grocery, for compensatory damages in the amount of $2,000, inclusive of medical expenses and lost wages;

b. Award pre-judgment and post-judgment interest as allowed by law;

c. Award costs and reasonable attorney fees; and

d. Grant such other and further relief as the Court may deem just and proper.
