COMPLAINT

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Emily Rodriguez, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant Somerville Grocers, Inc., alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff, Emily Rodriguez, is a resident of Middlesex County, Massachusetts.

2. Defendant, Somerville Grocers, Inc., is a corporation licensed to do business in the State of Massachusetts with its principal place of business located at 123 Main St, Somerville, MA 02143.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Mass. Gen. Laws Ann. ch. 218 ยง 3, as the matter in controversy exceeds the minimum jurisdictional limit of this Court.

4. Venue is proper in Middlesex County as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at 123 Main St, Somerville, MA 02143, within this judicial district.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 16, 2023, Plaintiff was shopping at the premises of the Defendant's grocery store located at 123 Main St, Somerville, MA 02143.

6. While in the dairy aisle, Plaintiff slipped and fell on a puddle of spilled milk that had not been cleaned up or marked with any warning signs.

7. The spilled milk was clearly visible, with cart tracks going through it, and was a hazard to customers like Plaintiff.

8. Despite an employee of the Defendant being in close proximity and aware of the spill, no action was taken to clean it up or warn customers of the danger.

9. The employee admitted to noticing the spill "a few minutes ago" but failed to promptly address the hazard.

CAUSES OF ACTION

10. Plaintiff hereby alleges a cause of action for negligence against the Defendant based on the following:

a. The Defendant owed a duty of care to Plaintiff as a business invitee to maintain its premises in a safe condition.
b. The Defendant breached its duty by allowing a hazardous condition to exist on its premises.
c. The Defendant's breach of duty was the proximate cause of Plaintiff's injuries.
d. As a direct result of the Defendant's negligence, Plaintiff sustained a hip sprain requiring medical treatment and incurring medical expenses.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff prays for judgment against the Defendant as follows:

1. Compensatory damages in the amount of $1,200 for medical expenses.
2. Compensatory damages in the amount of $800 for lost wages.
3. Costs and expenses incurred in prosecuting this action.
4. Such other and further relief as the Court deems just and proper.

DEMAND FOR JURY TRIAL

Plaintiff demands a trial by jury on all issues so triable.
