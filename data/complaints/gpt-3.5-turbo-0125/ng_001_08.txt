FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On September 16, 2023, Plaintiff was shopping at Defendant's grocery store located at 123 Somerville Avenue, Somerville, MA.

6. While in the dairy aisle, Plaintiff slipped and fell on a large puddle of spilled milk that was not marked with any warning signs or cones to alert customers of the hazard.

7. Despite an employee of the store noticing the spill minutes before Plaintiff's fall, no immediate action was taken to clean or cordon off the area.

8. As a result of the fall, Plaintiff suffered a hip sprain that required medical treatment, including physical therapy.

9. Plaintiff incurred approximately $1,200 in medical expenses and lost approximately $800 in wages as a direct result of the injuries sustained from the fall.

10. Defendant breached its duty of care owed to Plaintiff by failing to promptly address and warn of the hazardous condition on its premises.

11. The negligence of Defendant in failing to maintain a safe environment directly caused Plaintiff's injuries and resulting damages.

CAUSES OF ACTION

12. Plaintiff alleges that Defendant was negligent in failing to maintain its premises in a safe condition, failing to warn of the dangerous condition, and failing to take prompt corrective action in violation of Massachusetts negligence law.
