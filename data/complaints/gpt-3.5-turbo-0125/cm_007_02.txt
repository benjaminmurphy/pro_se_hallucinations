COMPLAINT FOR MODIFICATION OF CUSTODY

COMES NOW, Jessica Collins, the Plaintiff in this action, and for her Complaint against Michael Collins, the Defendant, alleges as follows:

1. PARTIES AND JURISDICTION
1.1. The Plaintiff, Jessica Collins, is a resident of Middlesex County, Massachusetts.
1.2. The Defendant, Michael Collins, is also a resident of Middlesex County, Massachusetts.
1.3. The subject of this action is the custody of the minor child, Samantha Collins, born on [date].
1.4. The original custody order was entered on May 14, 2019, by the Middlesex County Probate and Family Court.

2. FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS
2.1. The original custody arrangement granted the parties joint physical custody of Samantha, with a one-week-on, one-week-off schedule.
2.2. Recent developments in the Defendant's health have rendered them unable to provide adequate care for Samantha.
2.3. The Defendant has been diagnosed with a progressive neurological condition, resulting in physical limitations and an increased risk of falls.
2.4. Despite medical advice against it, the Defendant insists on maintaining the 50/50 custody arrangement and, at times, relies on Samantha for assistance that places her in precarious situations.
2.5. It is in Samantha's best interest to have her primary residence with the Plaintiff to ensure her safety, well-being, and proper care.

3. REQUESTED MODIFICATION
3.1. The Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court modify the existing custody order to grant primary physical custody of Samantha to the Plaintiff, Jessica Collins, and establish a revised parenting plan that considers the Defendant's health condition and abilities.
3.2. The Plaintiff further requests that the Defendant be granted appropriate parenting time that aligns with their physical capabilities and provides for Samantha's safety and best interests.

4. JURISDICTION AND VENUE
4.1. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter pursuant to Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 208, Section 28.
4.2. Venue is proper in Middlesex County as the minor child, Samantha Collins, resides within this jurisdiction.

WHEREFORE, the Plaintiff respectfully requests that this Honorable Court modify the custody order in accordance with the allegations set forth herein and grant such other relief as the Court deems just and proper.
