COMPLAINT FOR NEGLIGENCE

COMES NOW, Plaintiff Emily Johnson, by and through undersigned counsel, and for her Complaint against Defendant Mark Thompson, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Emily Johnson is a resident of Newton, Middlesex County, Massachusetts.

2. Defendant Mark Thompson is a resident of Newton, Middlesex County, Massachusetts.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This Court has jurisdiction over this matter as the incident giving rise to this action occurred at Newton Park, 123 Maple Street, Newton, Massachusetts, within the jurisdiction of the Newton District Court.

4. Venue is proper in this Court as the location of the incident and the residence of both parties are within its jurisdiction.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. On May 15, 2023, at Newton Park, Plaintiff Emily Johnson was jogging in accordance with park regulations which require all dogs to be on leashes.

6. Defendant Mark Thompson, knowing the park rules, regularly allowed his large dog to run loose in the park.

7. On the day in question, the Defendant's unleashed dog charged at Plaintiff while barking loudly.

8. Despite being approximately thirty feet away, the Defendant failed to control the dog by not attaching the leash, as he was seen holding it over his shoulder while engaged in conversation with friends.

9. In fear of being bitten by the aggressive dog, Plaintiff was forced to swerve off the path to avoid the animal.

10. As a result of the evasive action, Plaintiff tripped over a tree root and fell down a small embankment, fracturing her wrist.

11. The Defendant only called back the dog after Plaintiff fell, and the dog did not make physical contact with Plaintiff.

12. Multiple witnesses observed the incident unfold, verifying Plaintiff's account.

CAUSES OF ACTION

13. The Defendant's actions, in allowing his dog to run loose despite park regulations and failing to control the dog, constitute negligence under Massachusetts law.

14. The Defendant breached the duty of care owed to Plaintiff by allowing his dog to pose a foreseeable risk of harm to others in the park.

15. The Defendant's negligence directly caused Plaintiff's injuries and resulting damages.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Emily Johnson respectfully requests the following relief from this Honorable Court:

1. Compensatory damages in the amount of $4,700 for medical expenses and lost wages.
2. Prejudgment interest as allowed by law.
3. Any further relief that the Court deems just and proper.
