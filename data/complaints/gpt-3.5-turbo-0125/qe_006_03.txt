COMPLAINT

Now comes the Plaintiff, Jessica Rivera, and for her Complaint against the Defendant, Mark Thompson, alleges as follows:

PARTIES

1. Plaintiff Jessica Rivera is an individual residing at 45 Maple Street, Brockton, MA 02301.

2. Defendant Mark Thompson is the landlord of the aforementioned property located at 45 Maple Street, Brockton, MA 02301.

JURISDICTION AND VENUE

3. This action arises under Massachusetts landlord-tenant law and falls within the jurisdiction of this Honorable Court.

4. Venue is proper in the Brockton District Court as the subject property is located within its jurisdiction.

FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS

5. Plaintiff entered into a lease agreement with Defendant on June 1, 2022, to rent an apartment at 45 Maple Street, Brockton, MA 02301, for a monthly rent of $1200.

6. On [date hot water issue was reported], Plaintiff reported to Defendant that the hot water in her apartment had ceased functioning.

7. Defendant acknowledged the issue and informed Plaintiff that a part for the water heater needed replacement and was on order.

8. Despite assurances of ongoing efforts, Defendant failed to remedy the hot water issue for over three weeks, leaving Plaintiff without essential hot water facilities.

9. Plaintiff was forced to incur additional costs, including $30 per month for gym memberships for shower facilities, as well as increased expenses for gas to commute for showers.

10. Additionally, Plaintiff had to take her infant child elsewhere for bathing as a result of the prolonged lack of hot water in the apartment.

11. Plaintiff estimates damages of approximately $400 as a result of Defendant's failure to provide essential services as required under the lease agreement.

CAUSE OF ACTION

12. Plaintiff asserts a cause of action for breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment under Massachusetts General Laws, Chapter 186, Section 14, which guarantees a tenant the right to live in the leased premises without interference or disturbance from the landlord.

PRAYER FOR RELIEF

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff Jessica Rivera requests that this Honorable Court grant the following relief:

1. Award damages in the amount of $400 for the breach of the covenant of quiet enjoyment.

2. Any further relief that the Court deems just and proper.

JURY DEMAND

Plaintiff hereby demands a trial by jury on all issues so triable.
